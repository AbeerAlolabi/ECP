Our Members

The Education, Conflict and Peacebuilding (ECP) Interest Group includes graduate students, faculty, and practitioners, who are all exploring the intersection between education, peace, conflict, and development. ECP conducts monthly meetings that involve a deep and critical exploration into a current issue or topic within the field. Any participant can volunteer to facilitate and lead a monthly session or share their own research and practitioner experience with the group. We welcome anyone with an interest in this topic to join a monthly meeting or our mailing list by contacting us at  ecp.oise@gmail.com
